Aliquot Sequences: Computational Number-Theory

This project is a study in computational number-theory, focusing on aliquot sequences and properties of the sum-of-proper-divisors function. It includes an extension of the Pollack and Pomerance model which estimates the density of k-parent numbers. The project also explores the conjectures surrounding the behavior of aliquot sequences and attempts to computationally verify the proposed model.
